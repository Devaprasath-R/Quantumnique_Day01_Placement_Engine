Reading Passage

       new technologies to work better.

               1.Artificial Intelligence (AI)
               2.Machine Learning
               3.Automation

    3. Why are companies relying on data-driven decisions?
         Companies use data-driven decisions because data helps them understand information and
        make better decisions for their business.
