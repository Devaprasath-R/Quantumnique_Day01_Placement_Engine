BLOCK 1 APTITUDE (Time & Work)

Question 1

A can complete a work in 10 days and B in 15 days.
How many days will they take together?

Sol : 

      A = 10 days
      B = 15 days
 Total work = 30 units 
Work per day :
     A = 30 / 10
       = 3 units/day
     B = 30 / 15 
       = 2 units/day
       = 3 + 2
       = 5 units/day
       = 30 / 5
       = 6 days

Question 2

A alone can do a work in 12 days.After working for 4 days, B joins and work finishes in 4 more days.
Find B's efficiency.

Sol :

     A = 12 days
    Total work = 12 units
    Work per day = 1 unit
    Work done = 4 units
            = 12 - 4
            = 8 units
    Work per day = 8 / 4 
                 = 2 units
        B = 2 - 1
          = 1 unit/day
          = 12 / 1 
          = 12 

Question 3

If 5 people can complete a work in 20 days,how many people are needed to complete the work in 10 days?

Sol :
    5 people → 20 days
            = 5 × 20
            = 100
            = 100 / 10 
            = 10 

Question 4

A is twice as efficient as B.
If B takes 12 days to complete work, how many days will A take?

Sol :
     A  is 2 times faster than B
          B = 12 days
          T = 12 / 2 
            = 6 days

Question 5

Pipe A fills a tank in 6 hours and Pipe B fills in 8 hours.How long will both take together?

Sol :
     Pipe A = 6 hrs
     Pipe B = 8 hrs
Total work = 24 units (LCM 6 & 8)
        A = 24 / 6 
          = 4
        B = 24 / 8 
          = 3
          = 4 + 3 
          = 7 
          = 24 / 7 
          = 3.42 hr
